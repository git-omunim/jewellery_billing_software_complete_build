What is oMunim
------------------------------
oMunim (Online Munim) is complete accounting software which is specially design for jewellery and Pawn (GIRVI) business. It is a software application, which will excel your business and provide a complete analysis report for your business growth. Our expertise teams understand your requirements and provide the best solutions for your business. oMunim provides you a wide variety of features that will reduce your time and enhance your business revenue. It has several security checks for your data protection. oMunim has been designed as an online as well as offline web application that is very user friendly and easy to understand.
